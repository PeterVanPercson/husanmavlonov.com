Long · for feature article / about page 348 words

Husan Mavlonov is a 19-year-old founder from Tashkent, Uzbekistan, currently based in Shanghai. He is the co-founder and CEO of defex, an edge-AI defect-inspection company built on NVIDIA Jetson hardware for foreign-invested small and mid-sized factories in the Yangtze River Delta. defex was co-founded in early 2026 with his twin brother Hasan Mavlonov, who serves as CTO. The company's wedge is structural: pricing engineered to sit inside plant-manager discretionary spend (roughly $5,000 upfront plus $350 monthly), bypassing the overseas-HQ capex cycles that strangle the typical AOI vendor pitch. Its first paid pilot is targeted for August 2026.

Alongside defex, Husan runs BuildCored, a community of 1,500+ AI and hardware builders in China that he founded in 2024. BuildCored is funded by Uzbekistan's Youth Affairs Agency and runs cohort programs, technical events, and the Orcas builder-residency series. He is also a co-founder of mindform, a research project on persistent personality in LLM agents, with co-author Nuo Chen (endorsed for arXiv submission).

Husan is a Z Fellow (2026 cohort), a Lumiere Scholar, and a Türkiye Bursları scholar. He studies at the University of Michigan – Shanghai Jiao Tong Joint Institute. He has previously studied at Karabük University in Türkiye on full scholarship and at the Fudan University Chinese language program. He speaks English (C1+), Russian (native), Uzbek (native), Turkish, and Mandarin (HSK 1, improving).

Before founding defex he built and shipped EduRadar, a production Next.js + Anthropic SDK application for international student admissions, integrating Claude Opus and Haiku alongside the Semantic Scholar API. He has trained YOLOv8 baselines on public PCB datasets that now de-risk defex's first technical milestone. He writes occasionally about edge AI, manufacturing, and building in public on x.com/MavlonovHusan and on his personal site, husanmavlonov.com.

03Pre-formatted quotes

From Husan, on the record. Use as-is.
"The wedge for defex isn't tech, it's pricing. The plant manager has discretionary spend in their pocket. The HQ has a 9-month capex cycle. We sized the SKU to clear the first, not the second." Husan Mavlonov · on defex's positioning
"We didn't have to do founder-dating. The trust is 19 years old. The coordination cost is family-low. That's an architectural advantage you can't replicate." Husan Mavlonov · on co-founding with his twin
"Cognex skips these factories because they price out, not because the factories don't need inspection. Manual inspection at $7K a month is what they're actually paying. We're not replacing a $200K system — we're replacing five people." Husan Mavlonov · on the YRD wedge
"Edge-only isn't a feature. It's the only architecture that survives Chinese data-residency policy. Cloud-AI vendors don't get past the procurement conversation here." Husan Mavlonov · on the on-prem decision
"I love building. I'd rather ship a v1 that's narrow and real than a v2 deck that's broad and pretend." Husan Mavlonov · personal motto
